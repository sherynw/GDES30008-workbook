WEEK 5 RESEARCH

Yayoi Kusama's infinity castle artwork is a creative and unique play on mirrors that would change the perspective of the view into a large surrounding area despite being in a confined space which also gives an infinity never ending illusion to people. The colours are contrasted beautifully and the space filled with mirrors creates interactive layers within the whole 360 view.

Another reference I found quite interesting was the photobooth in apple devices where it create a different outlook from the connection between webcams where there could be effects or filters such as the threshold or distorted view all interactive through the video capture on webcam. It also has animated effects which tracks down movements of users that makes it a fun and quirky activity.
