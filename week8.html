WEEK 8 ACTIVITIES

RESEARCH

In Week 8, we worked on speculative objects into computing with sound, objects and touch sensitivity. We also look into real life example of physical inventions such as Chindogu that are made to "make life easier" in some sense.

In this week's class, there is a focus on introducing sound
towards the workshop activities where the system is touch
sensitive that produces different sounds, with the help of coding
in Arduino. Additionally, we experimented touch with different
objects such as foil and potato in creating other sounds.

Chindogu is a concept derived from
Japan where it is considered useless
inventions that could potentially
work but it might not be a practical
or efficient way. There are multiple
examples such as attaching the mop
scrubs to a baby's clothing to encourage
them cleaning the floor since they are
crawling everytime.
